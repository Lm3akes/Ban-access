1. Dashboard Vision
The Dashboard is the brain of the entire XmodzAntiban TV ecosystem.
Everything inside the application must be manageable without publishing a new application update.
The Dashboard should allow administrators to manage content, users, AI, servers, analytics, notifications, security, and system settings from one centralized interface.
